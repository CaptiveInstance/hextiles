The piece is a triangle of three hexagons. Take turns placing pieces on empty cells — hover to preview, press R (or the rotate button) to pivot the piece, click to place. On a phone: tap once to preview, tap again to place. When no piece fits anywhere, the game ends: whoever placed more pieces wins. Squeeze your pieces in, and waste space so your opponent can't.
