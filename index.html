Meet the hexafoil

Three hexagons, one piece. You and a partner take turns planting hexafoils to grow a shared garden — you plant teal, they plant coral.

Planting

Tap an empty cell to preview your hexafoil — it appears faded, like this. Tap the ⟳ Rotate button to spin it around the cell, then tap the same cell again to plant it.

Growing together

Every hexafoil must touch the garden, which grows outward forever. There's no score and no winner. One rule of care: you can't make a move that seals off a gap too small to ever hold a hexafoil — the game simply won't allow it.
